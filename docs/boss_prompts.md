# OVERCLOCK Boss Sprite Prompts

## Base Prompt Template

```
Create a pixel art BOSS MONSTER sprite for a cyberpunk idle RPG called "OVERCLOCK".

Boss name: [BOSS_NAME]
Sprite Tier: [1-3]

=== STYLE RULES (ALL TIERS) ===
- HIGH QUALITY pixel art - crisp, detailed, professional grade
- MINIMUM 64x64 pixel detail level (no blurry/muddy sprites)
- 32-bit retro pixel art style, clean edges, NO anti-aliasing, NO blur
- IMAGE SIZE: 512x512 pixels (ALWAYS - no exceptions)
- SPRITE SIZE: Boss centered, occupying tier-defined % of canvas
- Background: SOLID SINGLE COLOR (dark purple #1a0a2e or dark blue #0a0a2e)
- NO compression artifacts, NO noise, NO grain
- NO text, numbers, or symbols ON sprite body
- NO UI elements, health bars, labels, watermarks
- PURE CHARACTER ONLY - centered in frame, sharp outlines
- MUST look like a BOSS/THREAT/FINAL ENEMY - dominant stance, intimidating presence
- Larger and more imposing than regular enemies
- Sharp edges, menacing features, combat-ready posture
- Each pixel must be intentional - no random noise or artifacts

=== QUALITY REQUIREMENTS ===
- Crisp pixel edges - every pixel clearly defined
- Consistent pixel size throughout the sprite
- Clean color separation - no muddy blending
- Readable silhouette at any zoom level
- Professional pixel art quality - arcade/console game standard

=== TIER-SPECIFIC RULES ===
[Apply the tier characteristics below - SIZE refers to boss size within the 512x512 canvas]
```

---

## Tier Characteristics

### TIER 1

- Theme: Stage guardians, gatekeepers, first line of defense
- Vibe: Imposing but beatable, a real challenge but not overwhelming
- Personality: Confident, territorial, defending their domain
- Threat Level: Boss encounter - requires focus and strategy
- SIZE: LARGE (60-70% of canvas)
- COMPLEXITY: Detailed shapes, 4-5 colors, proper shading and highlights
- GLOW: Strong glow effects, pulsing energy core or eyes
- EXPRESSION: Aggressive, challenging the player directly

---

### TIER 2

- Theme: Elite commanders, system overlords, ancient threats
- Vibe: Dread-inducing, powerful presence, demands respect
- Personality: Superior, calculating, has seen many challengers fall
- Threat Level: Major boss - serious damage potential, punishes mistakes
- SIZE: HUGE (70-80% of canvas)
- COMPLEXITY: Intricate detail, 5-6 colors, particle effects, layered elements
- GLOW: Intense multi-colored glow, reality distortion effects around them
- EXPRESSION: Cold dominance, absolute authority

---

### TIER 3

- Theme: Final forms, godlike entities, system-level threats incarnate
- Vibe: Terror and awe combined, fighting something beyond mortal
- Personality: Inevitable, absolute - resistance is futile but necessary
- Threat Level: Ultimate challenge - endgame boss, overwhelming power
- SIZE: MASSIVE (80-90% of canvas)
- COMPLEXITY: Maximum detail, 6+ colors, multiple visual layers
- GLOW: Blinding intensity, corruption spreading, reality tearing at edges
- EXPRESSION: Beyond mortal emotion, pure destruction incarnate
